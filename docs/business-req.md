> _Answer:_

**1.2 — Do you ever go to them?**

**1.3 — Do you post racquets back to people?**

in person?

week"?

**1.5 — How quickly do you turn jobs around?**

## 2. How you want bookings to work

**2.1 — How do customers book today?**
Right now, how does someone arrange a restring with you? (Phone? Text?
Walk in? Email?)

> _Answer:_

**2.2 — Would you like the website to take bookings for you?**
Picture this: a customer fills in a form on the website saying what they
want, and it lands with you automatically — no phone call needed. Is
that something you want?

> _Answer:_

**2.3 — What do you NEED to know from every customer?**
When someone books, what must they tell you? For example:
- Their name and phone number
- What string they want
- How tight they want it strung (the tension)
- When they need it by

Is there anything else you always ask?

> _Answer:_

**2.4 — Do you want to be told the moment a booking comes in?**
Would you like a text message or email straight away when someone books,
so you don't have to keep checking?
